About Me

I am a driven individual with over 10 years of experience in hospitality, tourism and events industry. My passion is to enable others to deliver the best customer service using efficient and user friendly tools. The term ‘midway’ does not exist in my dictionary and when I commit myself I do so wholeheartedly.

Why I got into programming

When working in hospitality industry you do the best you can with the tools you have and more often than not the digital tools are outdated. This became very clear when I started working for an online fashion retailer- the systems used were time consuming to navigate and not easy to use.

My Mission

I am very passionate about what I do and I am going to change the world by providing practical programs which are beautifully designed and user friendly. Enabling people in the service industry to efficiently provide excellent customer service is my mission.

I graduated from Napier University in 2015. I received a 2:1 BA in Festival and Events Management with Entrepreneurship. Since then my passion for the industry remained but my goals have changed.

I have backpacked through most of Europe and a bit of Southeast Asia and Northern Africa. For now I have visited 28 countries and I am planning to visit a lot more! I am excited about getting to know new cultures and of course sampling the national foods from other countries!
